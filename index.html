Automatic Detection of Diabetic Retinopathy

Automatic detection of diabetic retinopathy utilizes computational methods to analyze retinal images for signs of the disease,
aiding in early diagnosis and treatment.
By leveraging image processing and machine learning algorithms,
these systems assist healthcare professionals in identifying key indicators of diabetic retinopathy,
improving efficiency and patient outcomes.
Ongoing research aims to enhance the accuracy and accessibility of automated detection methods,
fostering collaboration between computer scientists and medical experts for impactful advancements in diabetic retinopathy management.
